The company having 2 manufacturing plants and regular source of raw materials. The products are processed in SS machineries with advanced technology and is dispatched with Lab analysis.\u00a0<\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

The company is situated in the center of India, at Nagpur and factories are located in MIDC, Butibori, Maharashtra and MPIDC, Borgaon, Madhya Pradesh.<\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

The company has a team of motivated professionals having experience of more than three decades.<\/p>

The company is a private limited concern managed by whole time Directors and a dedicated team of professionals:<\/p>
